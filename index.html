Book Club

Jonathan Livingston Seagull

This is a beautiful book about resilience, grit and determination. The author has metaphorically used the lives of seagulls to depict human emotions in a very crude and enchanting manner. What stands out for me, is the seagull named Jonathan, who goes against his flocks' tendency to just eat, breed and survive. He dares to fly. He fails numerous times but ultimately flies and tastes freedom.
